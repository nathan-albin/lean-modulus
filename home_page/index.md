This page tracks the progress of the [Lean
4](https://lean-lang.org/)/[Mathlib](https://github.com/leanprover-community/mathlib4)
formalization of some of my research on graphs, networks, and the modulus of
families of objects.

## Papers

See the [papers table in the README]({{ site.github.repository_url }}/blob/main/README.md#papers)
for the current list of papers and their formalization status.

## Dependency graph

Each node below is a definition or theorem from the blueprint; colors show what's formalized,
what's ready to formalize next, and what's still blocked. Click a node to see its statement. Check the
legend for color coding. You can also view the [full-page version]({{ site.url }}/blueprint/dep_graph_document.html).
This graph, the blueprint pages it's embedded in, and the proved/`sorry` tracking throughout this
site are all generated by [leanblueprint](https://github.com/PatrickMassot/leanblueprint).
